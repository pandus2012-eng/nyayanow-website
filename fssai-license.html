What is FSSAI License?

FSSAI (Food Safety and Standards Authority of India) license is mandatory for all food businesses in India. It ensures that your food business meets safety and quality standards. The 14-digit license number must be displayed at your business premises and on all food packaging.

FSSAI ಪರವಾನಗಿಯು ಭಾರತದಲ್ಲಿನ ಎಲ್ಲಾ ಆಹಾರ ವ್ಯವಹಾರಗಳಿಗೆ ಕಡ್ಡಾಯವಾಗಿದೆ.

Penalty Warning

Operating a food business without FSSAI license attracts penalty up to ₹5 lakhs and possible jail time up to 6 months. License is MANDATORY before starting operations.

Who Needs FSSAI License?

  • Restaurants & Cafes - All eateries, food courts, QSRs, fine dining establishments
  • Cloud Kitchens - Swiggy, Zomato, online food delivery businesses
  • Home Bakers & Caterers - Home-based food preparation and catering services
  • Food Manufacturers - Packaged foods, beverages, snacks production
  • Grocery & Retail - Stores selling packaged or loose food items
  • Bakeries & Sweet Shops - All bakery products and mithai shops
  • Food Storage & Distribution - Warehouses, cold storage, food transport
  • E-commerce Food Sellers - Amazon, Flipkart food sellers

Frequently Asked Questions

Is FSSAI license mandatory for home-based food business?
Yes! Even home-based food businesses, home bakers, and cloud kitchens need FSSAI license. If your annual turnover is below ₹12 lakhs, you need Basic Registration (₹2,999). Many home chefs selling through Swiggy, Zomato, or Instagram need this license.
Which type of FSSAI license do I need?
It depends on your annual turnover: Basic Registration for < ₹12 lakhs (small businesses, home kitchens), State License for ₹12L - ₹20 crores (restaurants, manufacturers), Central License for > ₹20 crores (large manufacturers, national chains). We'll help you choose the right type during consultation.
Do I need FSSAI for a grocery store?
Yes, if you sell any food items - packaged or loose. Even kirana stores selling biscuits, snacks, or groceries need FSSAI Basic Registration. The license must be displayed at your shop. Operating without it can result in heavy penalties.
What is the validity of FSSAI license?
You can choose validity from 1 to 5 years at the time of application. Longer validity means lower per-year cost. Most businesses opt for 1 year initially, then renew for 5 years. Renewal must be done before expiry date. We offer FSSAI renewal service at ₹2,499.
What happens during the inspection for State license?
For State and Central licenses, FSSAI officer visits your premises to check: cleanliness, kitchen layout, food storage, water source, waste disposal, and staff hygiene practices. Don't worry - we provide a complete checklist and consultation before inspection to ensure you're ready. Most businesses pass on first inspection with our guidance.
Can I start my business before getting FSSAI license?
No! You MUST have FSSAI license before starting food business operations. Selling food without license is illegal and attracts penalty up to ₹5 lakhs. However, you can apply for the license and use the application acknowledgment number temporarily while waiting for the actual license (only for Basic Registration).
Do I need separate licenses for multiple branches?
Yes, you need separate FSSAI license for each business location/branch. Each premises must display its own license. We offer additional branch license service at ₹2,999 per location. If you have multiple branches in different states, you'll need State/Central license.
What if my turnover increases after getting Basic Registration?
If your turnover exceeds ₹12 lakhs, you must upgrade from Basic Registration to State License within 30 days. We handle the upgrade process at ₹3,999. Continuing with Basic Registration after crossing threshold can result in penalties. Monitor your turnover and upgrade proactively.
