What is Private Limited Company?

A Private Limited Company is the most popular business structure in India, offering limited liability protection to its shareholders. It's ideal for startups and growing businesses seeking funding or planning to scale operations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many directors do I need?
Minimum 2 directors are required to register a Private Limited Company. You can have up to 15 directors without passing a special resolution. At least one director must be an Indian resident.
Can I register from anywhere in Karnataka?
Yes! We serve all districts of Karnataka including Bengaluru, Mysuru, Mandya, Hubli, Mangalore, and more. The registered office can be anywhere in India, and the entire process can be completed online.
What is the minimum capital required?
There is no minimum capital requirement since 2015. You can start a Private Limited Company with as little as ₹1,000 authorized capital. However, most companies start with ₹1 lakh to ₹10 lakhs capital.
Can foreigners be directors?
Yes, foreigners can be directors and shareholders. However, at least one director must be an Indian resident. Foreign directors will need to obtain DIN and can participate through video conferencing for board meetings.
Do I need a physical office?
Yes, you need a registered office address with valid proof (rent agreement or property documents). It can be a residential address if you have NOC from the owner. Virtual offices are also acceptable in some cases.
What if my company name is not available?
Don't worry! We conduct a free name search before filing. If your desired name is taken or too similar to existing companies, we'll suggest alternatives that match your business vision and are likely to get approved.
Can I change the company name later?
Yes, you can change the company name later through a special resolution. However, it involves additional government fees (₹1,000) and takes 15-20 days. We recommend choosing the right name from the start.
Is GST registration included?
No, GST registration is a separate process and not included in the ₹6,999 package. However, we offer GST registration at just ₹999. We can do both registrations simultaneously to save time.
