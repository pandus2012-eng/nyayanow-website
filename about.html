About NyayaNow

NyayaNow is a digital legal platform built to bridge the gap between complex law and real people – citizens, law students and practising lawyers.

We focus on clarity, bilingual content (English & Kannada) and practical tools that help you move from confusion to action.

Our Vision

To make justice information accessible, understandable and actionable for every person, regardless of background or legal education.

Our Values

  • Clarity over jargon
  • Ethics over shortcuts
  • Education over fear
