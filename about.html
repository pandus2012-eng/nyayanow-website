NyayaNow ಬಗ್ಗೆ

ಕಾನೂನು ಮತ್ತು ವೃತ್ತಿಪರ ಸಹಾಯವನ್ನು ಸರಳ ಮತ್ತು ಪಾರದರ್ಶಕವಾಗಿಸುವುದು.

NyayaNow is a technology facilitation platform that connects citizens with independent Advocates and Chartered Accountants across Karnataka.

We simplify access to legal and financial assistance through a transparent booking process and a fixed platform facilitation fee.

NyayaNow does not provide legal, tax, or professional advice directly. All services are offered independently by registered professionals.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between citizens and legal professionals using technology.

Our Vision

To become a trusted legal facilitation platform across India.
