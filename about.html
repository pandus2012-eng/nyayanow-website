NyayaNow ಬಗ್ಗೆ

ಕಾನೂನು ಮತ್ತು ಹಣಕಾಸು ಸಹಾಯವನ್ನು ಸುಲಭ ಹಾಗೂ ಕೈಗೆಟುಕುವಂತೆ ಮಾಡುವುದು.

NyayaNow is a technology facilitation platform that connects citizens with independent Advocates, Chartered Accountants, and legal professionals across Karnataka.

Our platform simplifies access to legal and financial assistance through a transparent booking process and a fixed platform fee.

NyayaNow does not provide legal or professional services directly. All services are offered independently by registered professionals.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between citizens and legal professionals using technology.

Our Vision

To become a trusted legal facilitation platform across India.
