Our Story

NyayaNow was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to make legal help accessible and affordable for every Indian citizen. We recognized that millions of people across India struggle to understand their legal rights, navigate complex procedures, and access timely legal guidance.

Legal issues don't wait for office hours or expensive consultations. Whether it's filing an FIR, understanding a new law, drafting a legal notice, or seeking consumer rights protection, every citizen deserves quick, reliable, and affordable legal guidance.

That's why we created NyayaNow - a platform that combines legal expertise with modern technology to provide instant guidance through WhatsApp, making legal help as accessible as sending a message to a friend.

Our Mission

To democratize legal knowledge and services in India by providing affordable, accessible, and timely legal guidance to every citizen, regardless of their location or economic background.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted platform for legal awareness and guidance, empowering millions of citizens to understand and exercise their legal rights confidently.
