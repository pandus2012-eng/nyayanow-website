What is GST Registration?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) registration is mandatory for businesses with turnover above ₹40 lakhs for goods (₹20 lakhs for services). GSTIN is a unique 15-digit identification number issued to every business registered under GST.

GST (ಸರಕು ಮತ್ತು ಸೇವೆ ತೆರಿಗೆ) ನೋಂದಣಿಯು ವಹಿವಾಟು ₹40 ಲಕ್ಷಗಳಿಗಿಂತ ಹೆಚ್ಚಿರುವ ವ್ಯವಹಾರಗಳಿಗೆ ಕಡ್ಡಾಯವಾಗಿದೆ.

Important

Late registration penalty: ₹10,000 or 10% of tax due (whichever is higher). Register before you cross the threshold!

Who Needs GST Registration?

  • High turnover businesses - Mandatory if turnover exceeds ₹40 lakhs (goods) or ₹20 lakhs (services)
  • Inter-state suppliers - Mandatory regardless of turnover for interstate sales
  • E-commerce sellers - Required for selling on Amazon, Flipkart, etc.
  • Input Tax Credit seekers - Businesses wanting to claim ITC on purchases
  • Companies & LLPs - Mandatory for all companies/LLPs regardless of turnover
  • Reverse charge suppliers - Those receiving services from unregistered suppliers

Frequently Asked Questions

Is GST registration mandatory for my business?
GST registration is mandatory if: (1) Your annual turnover exceeds ₹40 lakhs for goods or ₹20 lakhs for services, (2) You make inter-state sales (regardless of turnover), (3) You're registered as a company/LLP, (4) You're an e-commerce seller, or (5) You want to claim Input Tax Credit.
How long is GSTIN valid?
GSTIN is valid as long as your business is operational. There is no renewal required. However, you must file regular GST returns (monthly/quarterly) to keep it active. If you stop your business, you should voluntarily cancel your GST registration.
Can I get GST without a physical office?
Yes, you can use any address as your business address - even your home address. You need to provide valid address proof (rent agreement or property documents) and NOC from the owner if you're renting. Co-working spaces and virtual offices are also acceptable.
What is the penalty for late GST registration?
The penalty for late registration is ₹10,000 or 10% of the tax due (whichever is higher) under CGST Act, and another ₹10,000 or 10% under SGST Act. This can add up to ₹20,000 minimum. It's crucial to register before crossing the turnover threshold.
Can I have multiple GSTIN for different locations?
Yes, you need separate GST registration for each state where you have a business presence. Within the same state, you can have multiple registrations for different business locations. Each location will get a unique 15-digit GSTIN.
What is ARN and when will I get it?
ARN (Application Reference Number) is a 15-digit number provided immediately after submitting your GST registration application. You can use this ARN to track your application status on the GST portal. The actual GSTIN is issued within 3-5 working days after ARN generation.
Do I need to file GST returns even with zero sales?
Yes, GST return filing is mandatory even if you have zero sales (Nil returns). Failure to file returns can lead to penalties of ₹200 per day (₹100 under CGST + ₹100 under SGST) up to a maximum of ₹5,000. We offer monthly GST return filing at just ₹499/month.
Can I cancel my GST registration later?
Yes, you can voluntarily cancel your GST registration if you've discontinued your business, closed down, or your turnover has fallen below the threshold limit. The cancellation can be done online through the GST portal. We offer GST cancellation service at ₹999.
