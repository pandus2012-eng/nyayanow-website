Documents Required

Different requirements for each structure

Public Trust

  • Trust Deed (we draft)
  • PAN cards of all trustees (2 minimum)
  • Address proof of trustees
  • Address proof of trust office
  • Passport size photos
  • NOC from property owner

Society

  • MOA & Rules (we draft)
  • PAN cards of all members (7 minimum)
  • Address proof of members
  • Registered office address proof
  • Passport size photos of members
  • First meeting minutes

Section 8 Company

  • MOA & AOA (we draft)
  • DIN for directors (2 minimum)
  • DSC of directors
  • PAN & Aadhaar of directors
  • Registered office proof
  • NOC from property owner
