Privacy Policy

NyayaNow is a technology facilitation platform that connects users with independent Advocates, Chartered Accountants, and related professionals. NyayaNow does not provide legal or tax advice.

This Privacy Policy explains how NyayaNow collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information provided by users and professionals.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as name, mobile number, email address, city, professional details, and service-related information. Information may be collected through our website forms, Google Forms, WhatsApp communication, or direct contact.

How We Use Information

Collected information is used only for platform facilitation, onboarding, communication, customer support, verification, and compliance with legal or regulatory requirements.

Data Sharing

NyayaNow does not sell or rent personal data. Information may be shared only with the relevant Advocate, Chartered Accountant, or professional connected to a request, or when required by law.

Data Storage & Security

Data submitted through Google Forms may be stored securely in Google Sheets with restricted access. Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are implemented to protect data from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss.

User Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their personal information by contacting us through official communication channels.
