FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and how we can help you.

Is NyayaNow a free service?

Basic guidance and awareness support are completely free. We only charge for optional add-on services like detailed lawyer consultations or document preparation ‚Äî and prices are always shared with you in advance. Our templates and basic legal advice are 100% free.

Is NyayaNow a law firm?

No. NyayaNow is a legal awareness and guidance platform. We help you understand your rights and options, connect you with lawyers where needed, and provide tools like templates and workshops. We are not a law firm and do not provide legal representation.

Can NyayaNow fight my case in court?

We do not directly represent you in court. However, we can help you understand the legal process, prepare basic documents, and connect you with qualified legal professionals if required for court representation.

Which legal issues can I ask about?

You can ask about police/FIR procedures, cyber fraud complaints, social media issues, tenant/landlord disputes, basic family matters, consumer complaints, RTI applications, women safety laws, and other common day-to-day legal questions. For complex litigation, we can connect you with appropriate lawyers.

In which languages do you provide support?

We currently support English and Kannada, and we're gradually adding more Indian languages. You can always write in simple language ‚Äî we'll do our best to understand and help you.

How long does it take to get a response?

For basic queries via WhatsApp, we typically respond within a few hours during business hours. For detailed consultations, you can expect a comprehensive response within 24 hours. Urgent matters are prioritized.
